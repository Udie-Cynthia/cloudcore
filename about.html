Teaching cloud with clarity and care.

CloudCore is a beginner-friendly learning space curated by Cynthia Udie, focused on turning complex cloud topics into practical, understandable lessons.

Our Mission

Empower beginners to build strong cloud fundamentals — with structure, empathy, and hands-on practice. We prioritize clarity, accuracy, and real-world relevance.

Our Approach

  • Explain, don’t intimidate: plain language, visual aids, and examples.
  • Practice: guided labs and exercises to cement understanding.
  • Pace: learn step-by-step with milestones you can track.

Meet Cynthia Udie

Cynthia is the educator behind CloudCore. She’s passionate about helping newcomers gain confidence with cloud concepts by breaking them down into friendly, structured lessons.

Her teaching style blends fundamentals with hands-on practice, so you not only understand the cloud — you can use it.
